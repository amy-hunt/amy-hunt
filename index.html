Grumpy Billionaire Ex

When my grumpy billionaire Ex rolls into Harmony Creek, my cozy little artsy world gets a whole lot messier. Ex-Navy SEAL Ethan bought our family winery in town and is back for much more. This arrogant jerk probably doesn't know the difference between a Merlot and a Mimosa. Working with him is torture; he thinks he can claim everything he finds, but he's wrong. About Amy Hunt, Romance Author

Welcome! My name is Amy Hunt, a best-selling romance author based in sunny Florida. Living in a cozy cottage near the beach with my husband, I draw inspiration from romantic books and movies. My writing is fueled by the joy of crafting compelling love stories featuring billionaires, small-town settings, alpha males, and sassy heroines. Each novel promises a happily ever after, perfect for fans of contemporary romance. Follow me to stay updated on new releases and exclusive content!

Quick look

When my grumpy billionaire Ex rolls into Harmony Creek, my cozy little artsy world gets a whole lot messier.

Ex-Navy SEAL Ethan bought our family winery in town and is back for much more.

This arrogant jerk probably doesn't know the difference between a Merlot and a Mimosa.

Working with him is torture; he thinks he can claim everything he finds, but he's wrong.

But his unexpected kiss caught me entirely off guard, reigniting a rush of familiar sensations. I never anticipated longing for him—the warmth of his touch, the tender press of his lips—

Should I fight to save my family's legacy or surrender to the allure of a happily ever after?

About the Author

Welcome! My name is Amy Hunt, and I live in sunny Florida with my husband. We live in a cozy cottage near the beach and often take walks there. I've always been captivated by romantic books and movies. My writing is driven by the joy of crafting love stories and thrilling plots and the privilege of sharing them with you. My billionaire, small-town, alfa male, sassy female, and fake relationship stories are inspiring and compelling, and they promise a happily ever after… If you're a fan of contemporary romance, follow me on this page and be among the first to learn about my upcoming releases. The anticipation is half the fun!
